Creative trouble, focused

404 KiDZ is where the weird idea gets a place to breathe. It is built for youth who are filming on phones, drawing logos in notebooks, making beats after school, editing clips, customizing clothes, skating spots, and learning how to turn taste into a real project.

The mission is simple: give young creators a lane, a challenge, a showcase, and a reason to keep building. The world does not need more permission slips. It needs more places where raw talent can get sharpened.
